    Biotechnology - Food Agroindustry

      Introduction

      The specialty in Food Agroindustry at the Benjamín Herrera Technical College aims to train high school graduates with skills in food production. The purpose is to guarantee the production of safe and high-quality food products from the transformation of raw materials such as fruits, vegetables, bakery products, meats, and dairy products. The program is carried out during the tenth and eleventh grades of technical secondary education. Four lines of work are taught: fruits, vegetables, and baking in the first year, and meats and dairy products in the second year. In addition, to obtain certification from SENA and a degree from the college, students of this specialty must complete a practical stage in companies in the agroindustrial sector.

      Objective

      Train high school graduates at the Benjamín Herrera Technical College for the agribusiness sector food production through the development of specific work skills capable of performing successfully in the workplace and its continuity in higher education.

      Student profile

    • ♦Maintain excellent personal hygiene

      ♦ Supervise the operations to which the food is subjected.

      ♦ Maintain the quality of the materials object of the process, through the correct application of the control systems designed for raw materials, the process, and the finished product.

      ♦ Properly operate the process control instruments to maintain the desired conditions.

      ♦ Evaluate according to the characteristics of the desired product and the raw material to be processed the appropriate ranges for the variables involved in the process.

      ♦ Estimate the degree of quality and safety of the foods that are processed to guarantee the health of consumers.

      ♦ Adopt new technologies, deep learning, interaction with all sectors of production and strengthen interpersonal relationships in the performance work environment.

      • Occupational field

      The Food Agroindustry Technician will be able to work in industries related to the processing and transformation of raw materials and inputs for the fruit, bakery, meat, and dairy lines, and is also trained in the areas of process control operator and processing machines. of food and beverages.
